MONKEY-TRAILS

A "monkey trail" can refer to several things: a play structure for children, a hiking trail, or a specific trail in Ao Nang, Thailand. It can also be a general term for a beaten path used by children in Western Canada.

1. Play Structure (KOMPAN): KOMPAN offers "Monkey Trails" as play structures designed to encourage children's physical development through climbing, crawling, balancing, and swinging.

Hiking Trails:

  • General Meaning:"Monkey trail" can refer to a hiking trail, often one that is somewhat challenging and features unique elements like wooden boardwalks or stairs, as seen in Ao Nang.
  • Ao Nang: The Monkey Trail in Ao Nang, Thailand, is a popular hiking route that includes a wooden boardwalk with stairs, leading to views of the beach and surrounding area.
  • Gibraltar:The Monkey Trail in Gibraltar's Nature Reserve allows visitors to see Barbary macaques.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The monkey trails are east west from the picnic sport.
